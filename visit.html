Getting Here

Address: 6200 Hermann Park Dr, Houston, TX 77030
Phone: (713) 533-6500

Located in the heart of Hermann Park, the Houston Zoo is easily accessible by car, METRORail, or bus. The Hermann Park / Rice University METRORail station is just a short walk from the zoo entrance.

Parking & Accessibility

  • On-site parking garage available for $15 per vehicle (cash & card accepted)
  • Free street parking available along Hermann Park Drive (limited availability)
  • Hermann Park has overflow lots during peak seasons and weekends
  • METRORail Red Line stops at Hermann Park/Rice University station
  • The zoo is fully ADA accessible with wheelchair rentals available at the entrance
  • Stroller rentals are available inside the zoo for $12/day
  • Service animals are welcome throughout the zoo grounds
  • Accessible restrooms are located near every major exhibit area
