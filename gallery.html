Animal Gallery

Explore stunning photos of the incredible animals that call Houston Zoo home.

Western Lowland Gorilla

A gentle giant of the African Forest. Our gorilla troop is led by a magnificent silverback who watches over his family with quiet authority.

Asian Elephant

Our elephants love their daily enrichment sessions. Watch them paint, splash in pools, and interact with their keepers during feeding demonstrations.

African Lion

The king of the savanna rests proudly in his exhibit. Our lion pair can often be seen sunbathing on the rocky outcrops of their expansive habitat.

Caribbean Flamingo

A flock of vibrant pink flamingos greets visitors near the zoo entrance. Their striking color comes from the carotenoid pigments in their diet of shrimp and algae.

Galápagos Giant Tortoise

These ancient reptiles can live over 100 years. Our tortoises are part of a vital conservation breeding program to protect this iconic species.

Red Panda

Often called the "firefox," our red pandas are skilled climbers who spend most of their time in the treetops, munching on bamboo leaves.

Masai Giraffe

Standing up to 18 feet tall, our giraffes are the tallest animals at the zoo. Visit the giraffe feeding platform for a close-up encounter!

California Sea Lion

Our playful sea lions are natural performers. Catch their training sessions where they show off flips, spins, and impressive underwater agility.

Blue-and-Gold Macaw

These intelligent parrots are known for their dazzling plumage and impressive vocal abilities. They can mimic human speech and live up to 60 years in captivity.
